In both the cases, the ‘from address’ is forged to make it appear as if it came from a source that it actually did not come from.What is Phishing? Phishing is an attempt by fraudsters to ‘fish’ for your personal details.

A phishing attempt is usually in the form of an e-mail, which encourages you to click a link that takes you to a fraudulent log-on page designed to capture your account/pword/personal details. These emails can also be used to lure the recipient into downloading harmful software.
